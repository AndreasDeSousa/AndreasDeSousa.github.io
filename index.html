New York City Taxi Fare Prediction

Machine Learning

We will train a machine learning model to predict the fare for a taxi ride in New York city given information like pickup date & time, pickup location, drop location and no. of passengers. This dataset is taken from a Kaggle competition organized by Google Cloud. It contains over 55 millions rows of training data. We'll attempt to achieve a respectable score in the competition using just a fraction of the data.
